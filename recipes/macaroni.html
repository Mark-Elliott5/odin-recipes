Macaroni

Description

This dish is an American favorite. It's simple yet delicious, and can be made in a very short amount of time. By itself, it's just cheese and pasta. But we can make it so much better with just a little more effort and ingredients. This recipe will make macaroni and cheese so good that you can never go back to the old stuff again!

Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil your macaroni in water with 1 tablespoon of salt for 18 minutes.
  2. While your macaroni is boiling, make a roux by melting butter in a pan, then adding flour, salt, and pepper. Stir the mixture thoroughly.
  3. Add cheese to your roux and stir until smooth.
  4. Drain macaroni and add the cheese sauce. Done!
