Spaghetti

 

 Description

  Spaghetti is a popular Italian dish that is easy to make just like macaroni and cheese. It involves noodles, beef, and tomato sauce. So simple, yet so tasty.

 Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce

 Steps

  1. Boil your spaghetti noodles in water with 1 tablespoon of salt for 12 minutes.
  2. While your noodles are boiling, take your ground beef and cook it in a pan. Make sure to break the ground beef up into fine pieces as you cook it with your utensil.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce to the ground beef and cook it a little more so that the sauce is the same temperature as the beef.
  4. Drain noodles and add the ground beef. Now it's ready to serve.
