Hamburger

 

 Description

  This simple placement of a beef patty changed between two buns changed America forever. It is more ingrained in American food culture than even milk. It's the food that people around the world think of when they think of the United States. It's the hamburger.

 Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Spices
  • Your favorite toppings

 Steps

  1. Take your ground beef and place it in your pan. Add spices to the beef and mix the beef thoroughly.
  2. Make a patty out of the ground beef and cook both sides until done.
  3. Place your cooked patty on a bun and add your favorite toppings. Ketchup, mustard, steak sauce, onions, pickles, lettuce, cheese... whatever you want!
  4. Eat!
